Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this reunion for?

This reunion is open to anyone who attended Hickory High School as part of the Class of 2011 — whether or not you graduated. If you transferred or your plans changed, you're still welcome.

For the Saturday evening dinner, each class member may bring one ticketed guest.

Can kids come to the Friday and Saturday events?

Friday: We're aiming for a family-friendly tailgate, football schedule allowing. If weather or the schedule require us to move to a bar or brewery, the event would be adults only. We'll confirm details as soon as possible.

Saturday dinner: Adults 21+ only.

Sunday picnic: Family friendly — kids are welcome.

Is there a dress code for Saturday's dinner?

TBD — check back for updates as we get closer to the event.

Do I need to RSVP for all three events?

Friday: No RSVP needed. Sign up at the bottom of the page to receive updates on the location and details.

Saturday: Yes — a ticket is required. Purchase tickets here.

Sunday: No ticket required, but please fill out the RSVP form to help us get a headcount and optionally sign up for a side or dessert.

What should I bring to the Sunday picnic?

We'll be providing pulled pork, hot dogs, and drinks. Use the RSVP form to sign up for a side or dessert to share.

Can I help with the event?

Yes! If you'd like to volunteer or help with any of the events, send us a message at hhs11alumni@gmail.com.
